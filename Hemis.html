Hemis National Park

Have you ever come across snow leopards? Well, Ladakh's Hemis National Park is considered to be the capital of Snow Leopard of India. What makes this national park exceptional is the fact that aside from inhabiting the famed snow leopard, it is also the only national park in the northern regions of the Himalayas. These cute snow leopards are begging you to visit this place. Back page
